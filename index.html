EBRAINS - Italy:
we're empowering a new era in Brain Research

EBRAINS - Italy is the Italian national research infrastructure for neuroscience, founded by European Union - PNRR M4C2. It provides access to electrophysiology, molecular, imaging, and computational tools that allows users to study and analyse human brain.
